## Overview

AI Sprite Generator uses cookies and similar technologies to keep the website working, remember preferences, support authentication, process payments, and understand basic product usage where analytics is enabled.

## Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are required for the product to work. They may include:

- Authentication and session cookies from Better Auth.
- CSRF and security cookies used to protect forms and account actions.
- Locale or preference cookies used to keep the interface consistent.
- Cookie consent records, if a consent banner is enabled.
- Payment session cookies or redirects used by Creem during checkout.

These cookies cannot be disabled through our product without affecting core functionality.

## Optional Cookies

Depending on the final configuration, we may use optional cookies or similar technologies for:

- Theme or interface preferences.
- Analytics and performance measurement.
- Email or support tooling.
- Affiliate tracking if an affiliate program is enabled later.

Optional analytics may include providers configured in the template, such as Vercel Analytics, Plausible, Umami, OpenPanel, Ahrefs, Seline, or DataFast.

## Third-Party Cookies

Third-party cookies may be set when you use related services:

- Google or GitHub when you log in with those providers.
- Creem when you open checkout or manage billing.
- Analytics providers if enabled.
- Hosting and security providers that help deliver the site.

These third parties manage their own cookies and privacy practices.

## Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Blocking essential cookies may break login, checkout, generation history, or account features. If a cookie preference center is enabled, you can also adjust optional cookie choices there.

## Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy as the product and service providers change. The latest version will be posted on this page.
